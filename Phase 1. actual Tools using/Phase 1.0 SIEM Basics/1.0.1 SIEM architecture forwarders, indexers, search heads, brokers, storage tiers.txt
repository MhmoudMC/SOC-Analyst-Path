1.The Logic: How the data flows, in professional SOC, logs don't just "appear" in splunk, they travel through a pipeline. even though you installed everything on one laptop, splunk is internally divided into three main workers:
1.The Universal Forwarder (The Collector): Think of this as small agent sitting on a server.
2.The Indexer (The Brain/Library): this is the most important part, it receives the raw text, add a timestamp, determines the "Source Type," and stores it in "Buckets" on my hard drive
3.The Search Head (The Face): This is what you see at //localhost:8000//. when you type a search, the search head asks the Indexer, "DO you have any logs about IP 1.1.1.1?" and than shows you the pretty results.
